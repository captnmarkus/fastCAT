Rewrite Threshold Checklist (for this repo)
Use this gate before moving backend services to Go/Java/Rust:

SLA gap: p99 latency exceeds target by >30% in 3 repeated runs.
Error budget: non-2xx + transport errors >0.5% at expected peak load.
Headroom: service CPU is >75% at peak while throughput is still below target.
Optimization exhausted: DB/index/query/caching/pooling tuning yields <20% further gain.
Workload fit: hotspot is CPU-bound (not mostly DB/network wait).
Economics: projected gain is at least 2x throughput or 30% infra-cost reduction within 12 months.
Decision rule:

4/6 true: rewrite only hotspot workers/services.
5+/6 true: consider full service rewrite.
